Frequently asked questions

When do I get paid?

[After the move is completed and the customer’s payment clears, typically within Net 30. — placeholder]

Do I need to track referrals myself?

No. We track referrals on our side using your unique link or code. You get a monthly statement showing referrals, status, and payouts.

What if my referral cancels or reschedules?

Cancelled moves don’t pay out. Rescheduled moves still pay when they complete.

Is there a contract length?

The partner agreement is open-ended. Either side can end the partnership with 30 days notice. No exclusivity required.

Can I refer commercial moves and long-distance moves?

Yes. All move types qualify. See the commission table above for rates.

Do you provide marketing materials?

[A short partner pack with logo, talking points, and link/code. — confirm with Mike on what’s included.]

Is there a minimum number of referrals to stay in the program?

No. Some partners refer once a year, some refer weekly. Both are welcome.

Is this the same as a referral discount for my client?

No. Affiliate commissions go to you, not as a discount to the customer. If you want to offer a discount instead of taking the commission, that can be arranged at signup.
