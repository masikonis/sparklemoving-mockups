Who we hire

Movers

Field crew. Loading, transit, unloading, placement. Most days are 6–10 hours. Crews dispatch from our Northbrook warehouse.

Drivers

CDL or non-CDL depending on route. Local routes year-round. Long-distance routes (Chicago to Florida, Chicago to Texas) for drivers willing to do multi-day trips.

Office

Quote desk, dispatch, customer service. Mostly weekday daytime hours.

Why Sparkle

You’re hired by us, not subcontracted. The crew that loads is the crew that unloads. Same applies internally — once you’re on the team, you stay on the team for the move. No swap-outs mid-job.

The work is steady. We’ve been in business since 2012 and run year-round, including a regular long-distance route schedule. Off-season slowdowns are real but limited.

The equipment works. Trucks, dollies, blankets, straps. All maintained, replaced when needed. You’re not improvising with broken gear.

The pay is the pay. No deductions for mistakes, no surprise reductions. What we agree on is what shows up on the paycheck.

FAQ

Do I need moving experience?

For movers: helpful, not required. We train. For drivers: yes, especially for long-distance routes. For office: depends on the role.

Do I need a CDL?

Only for routes that require one. Many local-mover positions don’t.

What’s the application process?

Application form → phone screen → in-person interview at the Northbrook office → trial day for field roles. Usually two to three weeks end to end.

Can I apply if I’m currently moving to Chicago?

Yes. Tell us your timeline in the application.

Is this seasonal work?

No. We hire year-round. Spring and summer are busiest, but the schedule doesn’t drop to zero in winter.
