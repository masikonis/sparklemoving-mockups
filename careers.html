Hiring process

From application to first day.

Usually two to three weeks end to end.

  1. 1

    Submit the application.

    Use the form below. Attach a résumé if you have one — we read every submission.

  2. 2

    Phone screen.

    15–20 minutes. We talk about the role, schedule, and what you’re looking for. You’re screening us as much as we’re screening you.

  3. 3

    In-person interview.

    At the Northbrook warehouse. Meet the crew lead, see the trucks and the gear, and ask anything still on your mind.

  4. 4

    Trial day (field roles).

    Paid trial on a real job. You see how we work; we see how you work. Either side can say it’s not a fit afterwards.

  5. 5

    Offer & first day.

    Written offer with the pay, schedule, and start date. First day is paid onboarding at the Northbrook office.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need moving experience?

For movers: helpful, not required. We train. For drivers: yes, especially for long-distance routes. For office: depends on the role.

Do I need a CDL?

Only for routes that require one. Many local-mover positions don’t.

What’s the application process?

Application form → phone screen → in-person interview at the Northbrook office → paid trial day for field roles. Usually two to three weeks end to end.

Can I apply if I’m currently moving to Chicago?

Yes. Tell us your timeline in the application.

Is this seasonal work?

No. We hire year-round. Spring and summer are busiest, but the schedule doesn’t drop to zero in winter.
