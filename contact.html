FAQ

I want a moving quote — should I use this form?

No. Use Book Online → or the Get a Quote button. The quote form takes about 60 seconds and gets your request to the right team faster.

My building needs a certificate of insurance. What do I send?

Email coi@sparklemoving.com with the building’s COI requirements (one-page sheet from the building manager). We file the COI with our carrier and confirm directly with the building.

Something got damaged on my move. How do I file a claim?

See the Insurance & Licensing page → for the full process. Short version: email claims@sparklemoving.com within 14 days with photos, move date, and the item.

Are you hiring?

See the Careers page →.

Can I visit the office?

By appointment. Most communication happens by phone or email. If an in-person meeting is useful, email us first to set a time.

Do you do consultations or in-home estimates?

For most local moves, the online quote is enough. For large homes, complex moves, or commercial relocations, an in-home estimate can be scheduled — mention it in your quote request and we’ll arrange a time.
