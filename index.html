Common questions

How does hourly pricing work?

You pay for the time the crew is working, from arrival to when the last item is placed. The rate depends on crew size — a 2-person crew costs less per hour but may take longer than a 3-person crew. Your quote shows the crew size, hourly rate, and estimated hours for your specific move.

What’s included in the price?

The crew, the truck, furniture blankets, shrink wrap for mattresses and upholstered pieces, and standard dollies. Packing materials (boxes, tape, packing paper) are available as an add-on if you want full-service packing.

Do you charge extra for stairs or long carries?

Stairs are part of the job — no extra charge. Long carries or elevator buildings may have a small additional fee. Any access charges are always disclosed in your quote before you book.

Can I get a firm price, not just an estimate?

For local moves, we provide a detailed estimate based on your inventory and move details. The final price reflects actual time, but our estimates are accurate. For long-distance moves, we can provide a binding quote.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Sparkle Moving is licensed by the Illinois Commerce Commission (Illinois CC-MC) and fully insured. Full licensing and insurance details are on our Insurance & Licensing page→
