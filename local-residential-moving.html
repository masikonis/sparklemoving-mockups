FAQ

How far in advance should I book?

Usually 1–2 weeks for a typical move. We take same-day and next-day jobs when crew availability allows. See Last Minute Moving →

What’s the minimum charge?

Two hours for a 2-mover crew, three hours for 3 or 4 movers. Plus the one-hour travel fee.

Can you move just a few items?

Yes. For loads under one truck or single pieces, see Single Item Moving →

Do you supply boxes?

Boxes, wardrobes, and tape are available. Full or partial packing is a separate service. See Packing Services →

Do you require a deposit?

No. You pay in full at the end of the move, once everything is placed.

What if my building requires a certificate of insurance?

Send us the building’s requirements when you book. We file the COI and confirm with the building before move day.

How is the travel fee calculated?

One flat charge equal to one hour of crew time. Covers drive time to your pickup and back from your destination. Billed once, not each way.
