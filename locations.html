FAQ

Do you charge more for moves in farther suburbs?

No. The hourly rate is the same everywhere we serve. The one-time travel fee scales with distance from our Northbrook HQ to your pickup and back.

How is the travel fee calculated?

One flat charge equal to one hour of crew time at your hourly rate. Covers drive time to pickup and from your destination back to base. Billed once, not each way.

What if I’m moving between two suburbs you serve?

Same crew handles pickup at one and delivery at the other. Hourly billing covers travel between them.

Do you serve downtown Chicago and the high-rises?

Yes. COIs, freight elevator bookings, and dock scheduling are handled by us, not you.

Where are your trucks dispatched from?

Northbrook, IL. Centrally located between the North Shore and the Northwest suburbs.

What if my city isn’t on the list?

We take jobs outside the list at a custom travel fee, depending on distance. Ask when you book.
