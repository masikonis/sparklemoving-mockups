FAQ

How is a binding quote different from a non-binding estimate?

A binding quote locks in price, weight allowance, and delivery window. It doesn’t change between booking and delivery. Most long-distance movers give non-binding estimates that go up at delivery. Ours doesn’t.

How long does a long distance move take?

Depends on the route. Florida and East Coast: 3–5 days. Texas: 2–4 days. West Coast and Pacific Northwest: 5–8 days. Your quote shows the specific window.

Will my stuff be on a shared truck?

No. Every Sparkle long-distance move uses a dedicated truck. Nothing else on it.

Do you offer packing for long distance?

Yes. Full or partial packing is available, quoted with the move. See Packing Services →

What if my dates change after I book?

Seven days notice or more: no fee. Less than seven days: depends on truck availability and route.

Is everything insured during transit?

Standard liability coverage is included. Higher-value coverage is available for high-end items, quoted with the move.
