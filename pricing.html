Hourly rates by crew size

Local moves are billed hourly, from the crew’s arrival to the last item placed. The rate depends on how many movers are on the job.

Crew size Hourly crew rate Minimum Typically used for
2 movers, 1 truck $139/hr 2 hours Studio, 1-bedroom
3 movers, 1 truck $169/hr 3 hours 2-bedroom apartments and condos
4 movers, 1–2 trucks $199/hr 3 hours 3-bedroom homes and houses

Rates are for the full crew, not per mover. Same rate every day of the week. No weekend or after-hours surcharge.

Real totals by home size

These are real ranges for local moves. They include crew time, truck, travel, and standard equipment. The range reflects how much stuff is in the home and how quickly the crew can work.

Home size Typical total Crew Hours
Studio / 1-bedroom $400–$900 2 movers 2–4 hrs
2-bedroom $800–$1,500 3 movers 4–6 hrs
3+ bedroom $1,500–$3,000+ 4 movers 6–10 hrs

Larger homes, long carries, or unusual access can push the total higher. Your quote shows the specifics for your move.

What’s included in every move

  • Professional crew
  • Clean, fully-equipped truck
  • Furniture blankets for wood and upholstered pieces
  • Shrink wrap for mattresses and soft goods
  • Standard dollies, straps, and hand tools
  • Disassembly and reassembly of standard furniture
  • Basic liability coverage (licensed and insured)

Fees and add-ons

The only charges that aren’t in the hourly rate:

Travel fee

One flat charge equal to one hour of crew time. Covers drive time to your pickup and back from your destination. Billed once, not each way.

Access fees

Stairs are free. Long carries over 75 feet from truck to door, or unusual access like freight elevators with wait times, are $25 flat. Disclosed in your quote before you book.

Packing supplies

Only if you want us to pack. Standard boxes start at $2.50. Wardrobe boxes $12. Full packing is quoted separately.

Overtime

Hours past the estimate are billed at the same hourly rate. No overtime multiplier.

Specialty items

Pianos, safes, and items over 400 lbs are quoted separately. See Additional Services.

What you won’t pay for

Not on your invoice:

  • No fuel surcharge
  • No weekend or after-hours premium
  • No gratuity required (tipping is optional, never assumed)
  • No rescheduling fee with 48 hours notice
  • No “heavy item” charge for standard furniture
  • No credit card processing fee

Long distance moves

Long-distance pricing doesn’t work hourly. You get a binding quote based on weight, distance, and truck space. Nothing changes between the quote and the invoice.

Common routes starting from:

  • Chicago to Florida: from $3,800
  • Chicago to Texas: from $4,200
  • Other destinations: quoted individually

  • Monthly storage: from $95/month for a small unit

Frequently asked questions

Can I get a firm price before I book?

For local moves, we send a detailed estimate with crew size, hourly rate, and hour range. Final price reflects actual time on the job, within that range. For long-distance moves, we provide a binding quote.

What if my move takes longer than estimated?

You’re billed at the same hourly rate for actual time worked. No overtime multiplier. If the crew finishes early, you’re billed for actual hours only.

Do you require a deposit?

No. You pay in full at the end of the move, once everything is placed.

How do I pay?

Cash, check, or credit card. No processing fee on cards.

Can I change my move date?

Yes, with 48 hours notice and no fee. Same-day changes depend on crew availability.

Is tipping expected?

Tips are appreciated, never assumed. Common range is $20–$40 per mover for a typical local move.
