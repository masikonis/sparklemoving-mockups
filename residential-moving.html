Frequently asked questions

How long does a residential move usually take?

A 1-bedroom typically runs 2–4 hours with a 2-person crew. A 2-bedroom runs 4–6 hours with 3 movers. A 3-bedroom house runs 6–10 hours with 4 movers. Your quote shows the estimate for your specific move.

Do you charge extra for stairs?

No. Stairs are free.

What if I live in a high-rise with a COI requirement?

Send us the building’s requirements when you book. We file the certificate of insurance and confirm the elevator booking before move day.

Do you supply packing materials?

Boxes, wardrobes, and tape are available. Full or partial packing is a separate service. See Packing Services.

Will I have the same crew the whole move?

Yes. The crew that loads is the crew that unloads.

What happens if my move takes longer than estimated?

You’re billed at the same hourly rate for actual time worked. No overtime multiplier. If the crew finishes early, you’re billed for actual hours only.
