How we handle a bad review

We’ve made mistakes. When we have, here’s what’s happened:

  1. We respond on the platform. Not behind a private email — in the same place anyone else can read it.
  2. We make it right where we can. Reroute a missed item, refund a clear overcharge, send a crew back.
  3. We leave the review up. We don’t ask anyone to take down a fair complaint.

If you’ve had a bad move with us and it isn’t resolved, email contact@sparklemoving.com directly.

FAQ

Why don’t you host reviews on your own site?

Self-hosted reviews can be edited or deleted without anyone knowing. Third-party platforms (Google, NiceJob, Yelp) keep the review attached to the reviewer’s account, with a date and history. That makes them harder to fake and easier to trust.

Are these ratings aggregated automatically?

Yes. The rating and count for each platform pull live from that platform. We don’t manually update the numbers.

What’s NiceJob?

NiceJob is a review management platform widely used by service businesses. It collects verified reviews from real customers and syndicates them to Google. Our NiceJob profile aggregates reviews requested after every completed move.

Do you have any awards or certifications?

We’re licensed by the Illinois Commerce Commission and registered with the FMCSA. Full credentials on the Insurance & Licensing page →. We don’t currently hold industry awards or BBB accreditation.

What if I had a bad experience?

Email contact@sparklemoving.com with your move date and the issue. We’d rather resolve it than read about it on a review site, but the public review process is open if direct contact doesn’t work.
